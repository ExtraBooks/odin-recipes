Avocado Toast

Description

This is another easy recipe that's great for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or even dessert. It's best when avocados are in season, but as long as you can find avocados, you'll be able to make this recipe.

Making the toast with different types of bread can also give it a different flavor. I encourage you to try creating this delicacy with a few different types of bread to find your favorite.

Ingredients

  • Avocado
  • Toast
  • Lime or Lemon Juice
  • Salt/Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast bread
  2. Spread avocado on the toast
  3. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper
