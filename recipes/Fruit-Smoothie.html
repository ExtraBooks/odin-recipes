Fruit Smoothie

Description

A very delicious and healthy drink that's great any time of day.

You can make this with fresh or frozen fruit. I enjoy frozen since it makes my smoothie cold without needing to add any ice.

Ingredients

  • Fresh or frozen fruit like berries, bananas, pineapple
  • Plant based milk

Steps

  1. Add the fruit and milk to a blender
  2. Blend and enjoy!
